Automation Test Engineer – Early Career

📍 Bangalore / Noida, India ⏱️ Full-time 👤 Fresher / Fresh Graduates
₹6,00,000 – ₹10,00,000 per annum

About the role

We are hiring fresh graduates as Automation Test Engineers to join our Quality Assurance Engineering team. You'll work on testing and automation for Adobe's cloud-based products, learning from experienced engineers and contributing to product quality across our suite of creative and business solutions.

This is an excellent opportunity to launch your career in software testing with one of the world's leading software companies. You'll gain hands-on experience with modern testing frameworks, work on real products used by millions, and develop expertise in QA automation.

What you'll do

  • Write and maintain automated test scripts using Selenium WebDriver, Cypress, and Playwright
  • Design and execute functional, integration, and regression test cases for web applications
  • Collaborate with development teams to integrate automated tests into CI/CD pipelines
  • Perform API testing using tools like Postman and REST clients
  • Identify, document, and report bugs with clear reproduction steps and expected vs. actual results
  • Participate in testing strategy discussions and contribute to test automation frameworks
  • Learn best practices in quality assurance and continuous improvement methodologies

What we're looking for

Required qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field (or recent graduate)
  • Proficiency in at least one programming language: Java, Python, or JavaScript
  • Understanding of test automation frameworks and tools (Selenium, Cypress, or Playwright)
  • Knowledge of API testing concepts and experience with Postman
  • Familiarity with SDLC and STLC (Software Development/Test Life Cycle)
  • Basic SQL knowledge for database testing
  • Strong problem-solving and analytical skills

Nice to have

  • Experience with version control systems (Git)
  • Exposure to Agile/Scrum methodologies
  • Experience with cloud platforms or Docker
  • Internship experience in QA or software testing
  • Familiarity with test management tools (TestRail, JIRA)

Why join Adobe?

Adobe is the global leader in digital experiences. We empower creators, marketers, and developers to bring their ideas to life. Our products are used by millions around the world to enable them to create, collaborate, and deliver better experiences.

As an early career engineer, you'll have the opportunity to work on products that impact millions of users, learn from world-class engineers, and grow your skills in a supportive environment focused on innovation and continuous improvement.
